We hear you. Change is hard, especially when what you're doing works... mostly.

Here's the thing:

  • Your players expect more now. They use apps for everything else in their lives. Modern convenience = more attendees.
  • Your volunteers deserve easier. You're asking people to donate their time. Give them tools that make that time less stressful.
  • Your mission needs more revenue. The old way leaks money through errors, slow throughput, and missed opportunities.

  • Phone support whenever you need it. Real humans who answer when you call, not ticket systems.
  • People actually prefer it. After one night, your team won't want to go back to the old way.

The system continues running offline. Everything syncs automatically when your connection comes back. You never stop selling.

You can use tablets you already have, or we'll help you get affordable equipment that works perfectly. Many groups spend $300-600 total to get started.

Yes! We offer a live demo where you can see it in action, plus a guided trial period. No commitment until you're sure.
What about our state's regulations?
We've worked with organizations in multiple states and know the compliance requirements. Our reporting tools are built to meet regulatory needs.
How long is the contract?
Month-to-month. No long-term commitment. If it's not working for you, you can cancel anytime. (But nobody does.)
